TREATMENTS

I take a full case history and carefully analyse your symptoms, to reach a proper diagnosis of your problem.

Assessment & Diagnosis forms an integral part of my approach to treating the whole body, not just a complaint in isolation. This service is fundamental to ensuring my patients’ long-term health and wellness.

There are many causes of low back pain ranging from a simple strain to a disc bulge or herniation. There can also be anomalies in the back such as spina bifida, scoliosis, primary shorter leg on one side or even a fracture.

A careful case history will outline your problem so that the appropriate treatment can be carried out.
Pain in the neck can eminate from a number of structures in the cervical spine ranging from disc problems, ligament strain which can result in increased muscle spasm and a range of aches and pains ranging from tension, headaches, dizzyness, pain in the shoulder blade area or pins and needles/numbness down the arm.

A careful examination of the cervical spine noting any trauma will give me an idea of the problem and how to give you effective treatment.
The lower three nerve roots join together to form the sciatic nerve, trauma to any one of these nerve roots can cause sciatic pain down the leg, as can an upper lumber strain which causes increased tone in the piriformless muscle. This puts pressure on the sciatic nerve causing pain down the leg.

Depending on the cause I will prescribe a treatment regime to alleviate your problem or symptoms.
Because your ligaments soften leading up to giving birth this can cause many aches and pains during or after childbirth. I will carefully analyse your movements and see where the problem emanates from. Treatments make allowances for your "bump" and is adapted specifically for pregnancy. After birth pains can be the result of your sacrum not returning to its normal position and osteopathic treatment is very effective at treating this and other painful problems.
No matter what sports you are involved in osteopathic treatment can quickly resolve any injury, remembering that different sports tend to have specific injuries. For example with skiing one tends to see knee sprains. With golf it tends to be the mid back which takes the strain.

No matter what your injury, a careful analysis will identify the problem and help me give you the appropriate treatment.
Whether you have a frozen shoulder, tennis elbow, knee pains, tempo mandibular joint pain or foot pain, a careful analysis of the cause and effective osteopathic treatment will help to resolve the issue and return the joint to its optimum movement without the pain.
